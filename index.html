About us

We are a collection of Psychology and Neuroscience graduate students who are interested in data science, user experience, and local beer. Our shared goal is to help each other prepare for a life (i.e. job) outside of academia, or perhaps take a more modern approach to one inside. You can read the latest blog post to the left, find older posts in the [archive]("/blog/"), or check out some of our [projects]("/projects/").
